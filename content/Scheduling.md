## Monthly
* Before a new month starts, create a [[Spreadsheet#^adf6b7|complete deck]] for each week and role, even if you are uncertain about who is available.
* As much as possible, schedule trainees or those who are still new to the role with other members who are more skilled so that they can learn more from them.
* Send the schedule to all members so that they can prepare beforehand. This also gives them the opportunity to let you know if they are unavailable on certain days.
---
## Weekly
- Confirm the availability of the volunteers of the upcoming week of their schedule and roles by privately messaging them.
- It may feel like bothering them, but it will often slip their minds. You are doing them a favor by ensuring that they don't forget.
- Get the tech script (a.k.a. program flow or liturgical flow) as soon as possible.
---
## Daily
- In case one doesn't reply, gently [[Spreadsheet#Message|message]] them again the next day. They may have read the notification but forgot to confirm.
- Find someone to fill the role in the event that a volunteer does not reply in time or is unavailable. You may do it yourself, or ask them to find someone to fill in for them.
---

>[!warning] Scheduling Conflict
> Some volunteers may be serving in multiple ministries which may cause scheduling conflicts. The monthly deck aims to avoid this by letting them block out their schedule beforehand. If multiple volunteers have schedules that clash with the same ministry, it is a good idea to coordinate with the ministry head or admin to avoid any hassle.
