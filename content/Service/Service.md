# During Service/Event

**SC**
- Computer running smoothly
- Camera angles
- Livestream/recording running
- Livestream/recording audio levels

**TD**
- Ensure things are prepared beforehand. 
- Avoid having to change or set things up at the last minute.
- Constantly monitor everything to ensure they are working properly.

- Do not allow things to get lost.
